First Round

For each match, pick a draw*, a winning team, or a team that will win by two or more goals on the entry form (before {{ 1402603200000 | date : 'MMM dd h:mma' }} (your time)). The table below shows how points will be allocated.

SO... If Spain were to play Portugal, you pick Span W+2, and Spain wins 2-0, 3-1, etc, you gain 6 points; if you pick Portugal W, and the match is a draw, you gain 0 points; if you pick Spain W, and Portugal wins 1-0, you lose 1 point and so on.

* This pool is only for the first round, so draws are ok. A new pool will be started with the "knockout round", the round of 16.
